CRISPY BONDA RECPES

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

8 servings

Tags : Breakfast Recipes lunch snacks Dinner

instructions

step 1

In a mixing bowl, combine the mashed potatoes, onions, coriander leaves, green chillies, ginger, mustard seeds, cumin seeds, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, and salt. Mix well to combine.

step 2

Shape the mixture into small balls, about the size of a ping-pong ball.

step 3

In a separate bowl, mix the besan, baking soda, and salt. Add water gradually and whisk until you get a smooth batter with no lumps.

step 4

Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.

step 5

Dip the potato balls in the besan batter to coat them evenly.

step 6

Carefully drop the coated balls into the hot oil and fry until golden brown, turning them over occasionally to ensure even cooking.

step 7

Use a slotted spoon to remove the bondas from the oil and drain the excess oil on a paper towel.

step 8

Serve hot with chutney or ketchup.

ingredients

2 cups boiled and mashed potatoes

1/2 cup finely chopped onions

1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves

2-3 green chillies, finely chopped

1 tsp grated ginger

1 tsp mustard seeds

1 tsp cumin seeds

1/2 tsp turmeric powder

1/2 tsp red chilli powder

Salt to taste

Oil for frying

1 cup besan (gram flour)

1/4 tsp baking soda

Water as needed
