SAMBAR RICE RECIPE

prep time

10 min.

cook time

50 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : Breakfast Recipes lunch snacks Dinner

instructions

step 1

Rinse the rice and toor dal together in a strainer and soak them in water for 30 minutes.

step 2

Heat oil in a pressure cooker or a heavy-bottomed pan, and add chopped onion. Sauté until onion turns golden brown.

step 3

Add chopped tomato and cook until it becomes soft and mushy.

step 4

Add chopped carrot, drumstick, green beans and peas, and cook for 5-7 minutes on low flame.

step 5

Add sambar powder, turmeric powder, tamarind paste, and salt to taste. Mix well and cook for a minute.

step 6

Add soaked rice and toor dal to the pan and mix everything together.

step 7

Add 4 cups of water to the pan and stir well.

step 8

If using a pressure cooker, cover the lid and cook on medium flame for 3-4 whistles. If using a pan, cover it with a lid and cook for 20-25 minutes or until the rice and dal are cooked completely.

step 9

Once cooked, remove the lid and let it cool down for a minute. Fluff the rice and lentils gently with a fork.

step 10

Heat ghee in a small pan, and add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Once they start to splutter, add dry red chillies and curry leaves. Sauté for 30 seconds.

step 11

Add the tempered spices to the sambar rice and mix gently.

step 12

Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with papad or raita.

Ingredients

1 cup rice

1/2 cup toor dal (pigeon peas lentil)

1 onion, chopped

1 tomato, chopped

1 carrot, chopped

1 drumstick, chopped (optional)

1/4 cup green beans, chopped

1/4 cup peas

1 tbsp tamarind paste

2 tbsp sambar powder

1/2 tsp turmeric powder

2 tbsp oil

Salt to taste

Water as needed

Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
