Ven Pongal

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : Breakfast Recipes lunch snacks Dinner

instructions

step 1

Wash the rice well for 2-3 times in running water. Then soak the rice and fenugreek seeds in water for two hours.

step 2

In a pressure cooker, add the soaked rice and dal, along with 3 cups of water.

step 3

Add cumin seeds, whole black pepper, grated ginger, and asafoetida to the cooker.

step 4

Close the cooker and pressure cook on high heat for 3 whistles.

step 5

Once the pressure has released on its own, open the cooker and mix well.

step 6

Add salt to taste and mix well.

step 7

Heat ghee in a small pan and roast cashew nuts until golden brown. Add the roasted cashew nuts to the pongal and mix well.

step 8

Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

ingredients

1 cup raw rice

1/2 cup split yellow moong dal

3 cups water

1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds

1/2 teaspoon whole black pepper

1/4 teaspoon grated ginger

1 pinch asafoetida

2 tablespoons ghee

Salt to taste

Cashew nuts (optional)
