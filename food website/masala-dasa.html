Masala Dosa

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : Breakfast Recipes lunch snacks Dinner

instructions

step 1

Rinse the rice and urad dal separately a few times in water and soak them in enough water for at least 4 hours.

step 2

Also, soak the fenugreek seeds along with the urad dal.

step 3

After 4 hours, drain the water from the rice and grind it to a fine batter in a blender, adding a little water as needed.

step 4

Similarly, grind the urad dal and fenugreek seeds to a smooth and fluffy batter.

step 5

Mix both the batters together in a large mixing bowl and add salt to taste. Stir well to combine and cover the bowl with a lid or a clean cloth.

step 6

Let the batter ferment overnight or for 8-10 hours in a warm place. The batter will rise and become airy.

step 7

Next, heat a non-stick tawa (griddle) or a flat pan over medium heat. Once it's hot, pour a ladleful of the batter in the center of the tawa and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin crepe or dosa.

step 8

Drizzle a little oil or ghee over the edges of the dosa and let it cook for 1-2 minutes until the underside turns golden brown and crisp.

step 9

Flip the dosa over and cook for another 30 seconds on the other side.

step 10

Place a spoonful of the potato masala filling in the center of the dosa and fold it into a triangle or roll it into a cylinder.

step 11

Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar on the side.

ingredients

1 cup rice

1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)

1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds

Salt to taste

Oil or ghee for cooking

Potato masala filling (made with boiled and mashed potatoes, onions, green chilies, curry leaves, mustard seeds, turmeric powder, salt, and lemon juice)

Coconut chutney and sambar (lentil stew) for serving
