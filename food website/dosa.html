DOSA RECIPE

prep time

2 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : Breakfast Recipes lunch snacks Dinner

instructions

step 1

+

Rinse the rice and urad dal separately in water until the water runs clear. Soak them in water separately for at least 4-5 hours.

step 2

In a separate bowl, soak the fenugreek seeds in water along with the urad dal.

step 3

Drain the water from the urad dal and fenugreek seeds and grind them in a blender with water until you get a smooth, fluffy batter. You may need to add more water as needed. Transfer the batter to a large bowl.

step 4

Drain the water from the rice and grind it in a blender with water until you get a smooth batter. Add this to the bowl with the urad dal batter and mix well.

step 5

Add salt to taste and mix well. Cover the bowl with a lid or a clean cloth and let it ferment for at least 8-10 hours, or overnight in a warm place.

step 6

After the batter has fermented, it will have risen and developed small bubbles. Stir the batter gently.

step 7

Heat a non-stick dosa tawa or a griddle on medium heat. Once the tawa is hot, pour a ladleful of the batter onto the tawa and spread it evenly into a thin circle using the back of the ladle.

step 8

Drizzle a little oil around the edges of the dosa and let it cook until the edges turn golden brown and crispy. Flip the dosa and cook the other side until it is cooked through and lightly browned.

step 9

Repeat the process with the remaining batter to make more dosas.

step 10

Serve hot with chutney and sambar.

Ingredients

2 cups of whole wheat flour

1/2 tsp salt

3/4 to 1 cup of water

1-2 tbsp of oil or ghee
