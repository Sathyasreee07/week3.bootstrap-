CHAPATI RECIPE

prep time

10 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : Breakfast Recipes lunch snacks Dinner

instructions

step 1

+

In a large mixing bowl, add the whole wheat flour and salt. Mix well.

step 2

Slowly add water to the flour mixture and mix well until a soft dough forms. Add water as needed, a little at a time, until the dough comes together and isn't too sticky.

step 3

Knead the dough for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.

step 4

Cover the dough and let it rest for 15-20 minutes

step 5

Divide the dough into 8-10 equal sized portions

step 6

Dust a rolling board or clean surface with flour. Roll each portion of dough into a thin circle or oval shape.

step 7

Heat a tawa or griddle over medium-high heat.

step 8

Place the rolled out chapati on the hot tawa and cook for about 30 seconds or until bubbles start to appear on the surface.

step 9

Flip the chapati and cook the other side for another 30 seconds or until light brown spots appear.

step 10

Brush the cooked chapati with a little oil or ghee and remove from the heat.

step 11

Repeat the process with the remaining dough portions.

step 12

Serve hot with your favorite curry or side dish.

Ingredients

2 cups of whole wheat flour

1/2 tsp salt

3/4 to 1 cup of water

1-2 tbsp of oil or ghee
