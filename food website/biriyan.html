CHICKEN RECIPE

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

8 servings

Tags : Breakfast Recipes lunch snacks Dinner

instructions

step 1

+

Heat the oil or ghee in a large pot over medium heat. Add the sliced onion and sauté until golden brown

step 2

Add the ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.

step 3

Add the chicken pieces and sauté until they are no longer pink.

step 4

Add the biryani masala powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.

step 5

Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot. Mix well with the chicken and spices.

step 6

Add 3 cups of water and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the biryani simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is fully cooked.

step 7

Once the rice is cooked, turn off the heat and let the biryani rest for 5-10 minutes.

step 8

Garnish with chopped cilantro and mint leaves before serving.

step 9

Serve hot with your favorite chutney or dipping sauce. Enjoy!

Ingredients

2 cups of basmati rice, soaked in water for 30 minutes

1 pound of boneless chicken, cut into small pieces

1 onion, thinly sliced

1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste

1 tablespoon of biryani masala powder

1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder

1/2 teaspoon of red chili powder

1/2 teaspoon of red chili powder

Salt, to taste

3 tablespoons of oil or ghee

3 cups of water
